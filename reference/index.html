Species

As time goes on, species evolve and become more advanced.

Species Description
Prokaryotes An extremely simple, primitive life form. There's not much special stuff about it. It doesn't use DNA, just RNA.
Archaea Like Prokaryotes, but with DNA and stuff.
Eukaryokes These are quite advanced. Their creation had to do with bacteria eating each other, providing each other as an energy source, growth, mitochondria, and other weird stuff.
Algae Yay! Eukaryotes have learned to work together to be more advanced.
Reptiles Dinosaurs and stuff like that. And fish. And stuff. I dunno.
Mammals Warm-blooded and stuff. And hair. Smarter. I think.
Monkeys Very very primitive human-like mammals.
Cavemen Primitive humans.
Humans We're already getting close to the next stage. Singularity shoud be complete by 2045, according to Ray Kurzweil. We're finally getting somewhere. We're going to soon reach Type I on the Kardashev scale.
--- Border between real and fiction, although things like AI could actually become true.
AI Unfortunately, these things could replace us. Like, by 2050 or something. At least they can reach Type II on the Kardashev scale.
Energy AI get impatient with the speed of light, and maximize energy in this universe by creating "Smart" collections of particles and energy that can produce wormholes and stuff. With this, the AI's Energy stage can easily reach Type III and Type IV on the Kardashev scale.
Creators Transcending our own universe, the Creators reach Type V on the Kardashev scale. At this point, they can manipulate everything in each universe. At this stage, they cannot yet reach outside of our multiverse, to access universes with different sets of physics.
Corruptors At this point, Creators become so fed up with the inability to advance to the next multiverse that they begin to destory everything. They destroy inconcieveable amounts of universes in search of the perfect universe, that contains the gateway past our own multiverse. When they reach this gate, they will become a Type VI civilization.
Protectors When they reach the gate, the Corruptors realize the damage they have done to their own multiverse. When they see this, they vow to not let anymore beings destroy as much as they did, to stop rogue civilizations. Now a Type VII civilization, the Protectors can begin to span across the multiverses to stop other civilizations that became like the Corruptors.
Infinities Now spanning all of time and space, within all of the multiverses, the Protectors have reached a state of peace. The Protectors incorporate themselves as part of the omniverse, and become infinite.
Seers With infinite power, the Infinities create and find new omniverses. They attempt to grow their power to find the perfect omniverse, to reach beyond, into new dimensions.
Shifters Finding the perfect omniverse, the Seers begin to ascend to an infinite state. They exist as a void, an infinity, energy, matter, everything. In the perfect omniverse, they find the gate to shift into higher dimensions.
Keepers The Shifters conquer all of the dimensions. At this state of being, they have truly infinite power. They can conceptualize everything. They do not conceptualize, they do. Now as Keepers, they maintain the peace and balance of everything.
